About Me

A determined mind
shaping her future

I am Sumaiya Jahan Alo — a Bachelor of Business Administration student at American International University Bangladesh (AIUB), majoring in Finance. I bring an interdisciplinary perspective to business, drawing from coursework in financial management, marketing strategy, and organizational behavior to approach real-world challenges with clarity and confidence.

My journey extends well beyond the classroom. Serving as a Campus Ambassador for leading brands such as PRAN-RFL Group, Lead Academy, Step Footwear, and The Front Page, I have built practical experience in brand representation, stakeholder communication, and grassroots marketing — skills that directly complement my academic foundation.

As Executive of Club Welfare at the AIUB Finance Club, I contribute to the well-being and development of one of the university's most active professional clubs. I have also earned recognition in multiple business competitions, including back-to-back championship titles — a testament to my research acumen, presentation skills, and strategic thinking.

I am driven by a desire to grow into a corporate professional who bridges financial intelligence with human leadership — someone who makes organizations stronger, smarter, and more people-centric.
